# 07 Submission Checklist

Before submission, confirm:
- article draft is complete in all required sections
- abstract is within the 200-word limit
- figures and tables have captions
- Results and Discussion are evidence-based and clearly separated
- Methods includes Colab, NVIDIA T4, dataset composition, augmentation, and Ultralytics workflow
- references are in Vancouver style
- adviser name is listed as required
- co-author or proponent naming is checked against the final instruction you choose
- article PDF is exported
- peer review statement is finalized
- Turnitin output is reviewed
- Grammarly output is reviewed
