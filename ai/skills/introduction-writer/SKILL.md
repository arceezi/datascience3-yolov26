# Introduction Writer

Write an Introduction that motivates the study and sets up the paper objective.

## Workflow

1. Open with the real problem:
- why road-user detection matters
- why the Philippine traffic context matters

2. Narrow to the technical framing:
- real-time object detection
- relevance of YOLO-based methods
- the study's specific comparison focus

3. End with a clean objective statement:
- what was tested
- what was compared
- what the paper contributes

## Guardrails

- Avoid calling YOLOv26 state of the art unless the paper or assignment explicitly requires that phrasing.
- Do not let the Introduction turn into a literature review dump.
- Keep claims about importance and application grounded and defensible.
- Do not preview detailed results here.

## Output Shape

Return:
- a polished Introduction draft
- a short note on any claims that still need citations
